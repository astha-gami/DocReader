SUBJECT: Aadhaar Address & Identity Update – Mandatory Compliance

Dear Resident,
This is to inform you that discrepancies were found in your Aadhaar demographic details during routine verification. As per UIDAI guidelines under the Aadhaar Act 2016, residents must maintain accurate Aadhaar information.

You are required to update your Aadhaar details on or before 10/03/2025. 
Failure to comply may lead to temporary suspension of Aadhaar-enabled bank transactions, DBT subsidy benefits, and PAN-Aadhaar validation.

Documents required:
1. Proof of Identity – PAN/Passport/Driving Licence
2. Proof of Address – Electricity Bill/Bank Statement
3. Recent passport-size photograph

Please visit the nearest Aadhaar Seva Kendra or update online at https://myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in.
