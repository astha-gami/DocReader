Aadhaar Update Notice

Ref No: UIDAI/RO-MH/NOTICE/2025/442 Date: 18/02/2025

SUBJECT: Mandatory Aadhaar Update for Address & Identity Verification

Dear Resident,

This is to inform you that during the periodic verification of Aadhaar records, discrepancies were
found in your address or identity information. As per the Aadhaar Act, 2016, residents must keep
their Aadhaar details accurate and updated for uninterrupted access to Aadhaar-enabled services.

You are required to update your Aadhaar details on or before 10/03/2025.

Failure to update may result in temporary suspension of:
« Aadhaar-linked banking services

* DBT subsidies

* Mobile number verification

*« PAN-Aadhaar linking validation

Documents Required (Any ONE each):

1. Proof of Identity (PAN Card, Passport, Driving Licence)

2. Proof of Address (Electricity Bill, Bank Statement, Rental Agreement)
3. Recent passport-size photograph

You may update Aadhaar through:

« Aadhaar Self-Service Portal (https://myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in)
« Nearest Aadhaar Seva Kendra

« Authorized Enrollment/Update Centre

Please carry original documents for verification.
